About Me

Hey! My name is Andrew Alsing. I've been tinkering with computers ever since I was 5. As a kid, I would bond with my Dad through helping him build computers and learning machine language. When I got older, I dipped my toes into python2 and made simple clicker games, rpgs, and scripts to help me with every-day tasks.
